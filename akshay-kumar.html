Akshay Kumar is one of the most successful Actors in the bollywood Film Industry . Since he is trained in martial arts so he is also known as the “Indian Jackie Chan “. In 2011 , Government of India awarded him ” Padma Shri” which is the fourth highest civilian award given to any indian citizen. According to “Forbes ” Akshay Kumar is the fourth highest paid actor in the world ( 2019 ) and the highest paid actor in India (2019).

For his contribution to the Indian Cinema industry , University of Windsor conferred an honorary Doctorate to him in 2008

Akshay Kumar is highly popular on social media platforms among his followers and fan. he has over 34.6 million followers on his Instagram Account.
